Biography

Mohsen is a computational chemist and software developer with over 75 ISI publications and 2 books, demonstrating measurable contributions to theoretical and computational chemistry. He specializes in molecular simulations, quantum calculations, and high-performance computing, leveraging Python, C/C++, Julia, and advanced data-driven workflows. Skilled in data engineering, analytics, and machine learning, he integrates scientific modeling with modern AI and cloud-based tools to achieve precise and reproducible results. Mohsen's work is goal-oriented: designing advanced materials for energy applications, developing selective molecular sensors, and contributing to drug design, while continuously enhancing efficiency, scalability, and predictive capabilities. His expertise bridges chemistry and the data ecosystem, ensuring impactful, relevant, and actionable outcomes in both research and software development.
